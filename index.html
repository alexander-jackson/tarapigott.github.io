ABOUT

As a recent 2021 Graduate of Global Sustainable Development from the University of Warwick, an interdisciplinary degree that explores a multitude of global challenges, I am motivated and excited to enter a career which works towards creating a more just and healthier world.

I am especially interested in the intersection of environmental elements of sustainability, with health; such as health equality, sustainable healthcare and sustainable diets and food systems. This lead to me undertaking my undergraduate dissertation on exploring the inter-disciplinary perspectives on diet for advancing sustainable urban food policy. I therefore have in depth knowledge of routes for food policy improvement and understand the barriers to successful policy implementation at local through to national levels.

Although I pride myself as a flexible, quick-learning, and multi-skilled individual, I believe my most significant skill set lies in communications. My strong organisation, meticulous nature, design skills, and experience running online campaigns, sets me up well to producing engaging and succinct communications. I would therefore excel at roles based in admin, internal or external communications, or campaign creation.
